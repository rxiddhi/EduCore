# EduCore LMS Project Idea

## Problem Statement
Educational institutions often struggle with disconnected systems for attendance, grades, and academic risk tracking. Teachers spend time on repetitive administrative tasks, while students get delayed visibility into their academic standing.

## Proposed Solution
EduCore LMS is a role-based academic management platform with separate portals for students and teachers. It centralizes course management, attendance recording, grade submission, and at-risk analytics in one system.

## Objectives
- Provide secure role-based access for Student, Teacher, and Admin users.
- Allow teachers to manage assigned courses, attendance, and grades efficiently.
- Allow students to track GPA, attendance, grades, and risk status in real time.
- Maintain audit logs for critical academic actions.

## Scope
- Authentication and role-based authorization.
- Course and enrollment management.
- Attendance and grade workflows.
- GPA and academic risk analysis.
- Dashboard views for teacher and student roles.

## Expected Outcome
A functional full-stack LMS that reduces manual effort for teachers, improves transparency for students, and provides better academic decision support through analytics.
